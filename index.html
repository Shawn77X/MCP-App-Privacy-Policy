Privacy Policy for Warp Facebook Page Integration

Effective Date: October 15, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Warp Facebook Page Integration app (“we,” “our,” “the app”) collects, uses, and protects data from Facebook Pages authorized by page administrators.

2. Data We Collect

With your permission, the app accesses:

  • Conversations (messages) sent to your Facebook Page
  • Basic Page information (Page ID, Page name)

No other personal data is collected.

3. How We Use Your Data

The data is used solely to:

  • Fetch messages from your authorized Facebook Page
  • Deliver those messages to the Warp platform for processing

We do not store, sell, or share your Page data beyond this purpose.

4. Who Has Access

Only the app and authorized Warp systems can access the data. Access is protected to prevent unauthorized use.

5. User Rights

You can revoke the app’s access to your Page at any time via Facebook Page settings or by contacting us at your-email@example.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

7. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us at your-email@example.com.
